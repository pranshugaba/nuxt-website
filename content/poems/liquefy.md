a late afternoon ---  
<nbsp :spaces="2"></nbsp>
chocolate and vanilla  
<nbsp :spaces="4"></nbsp>
cool delicious chocobar

but lo and behold ---  
<nbsp :spaces="2"></nbsp>
misfortune befalls  
<nbsp :spaces="4"></nbsp>
the ice-cream falls

liquefied dessert ---  
<nbsp :spaces="2"></nbsp>
will now be eaten  
<nbsp :spaces="4"></nbsp>
by a simian
