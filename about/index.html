About Me

I am a PhD student in Theoretical Computer Science at TIFR, Mumbai . I did my undergraduate studies in Maths at IISc, Bangalore .

My research interests include (but are not limited to) Automata Theory, Logic, Discrete Maths, and Algorithms.

I make photopoetry, create puzzles, draw comics, develop apps and games, and write stories.

I also love playing chess, solving puzzles, swimming and cycling but not both at once.
